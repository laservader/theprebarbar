Job Opportunities


Ambassadors

We are currently looking for motivated applicants to be the ambassadors of our product across campuses.

Expectations

  1. Motivation
  2. Sales Experience, or the willingness to learn
  3. Networking
  4. Growing sales into new areas
  5. Developing relationships with customers
  6. Comittmented to semesters of work at a time

Rewards

  • Payment will be incentive and commission based. This means the more motivated you are, the more you make.
  • Commissions will reach up to 50% of all sales you make
  • Additional cash incentives will be awarded to top salespersons

Enviroment

The idea is the give our ambassadors freedom to operate. You will have a vast amount of freedom when it comes to how you sell, and who you sell too. Additionally, you will commit on a semester basis, making it easier for students to work with us.

Promotion

For those who believe they can help us grow our brand on social media or else where, please contact us!
